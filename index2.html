  • About

    Sparring Partners is a new breed consulting agency, focussed on pioneering business practices for traditional companies and start-ups by rethinking existing processes & formalities.

    We believe that business should be conducted in a personal and practical manner, through unconditional acceptance of yesterday’s predictabilities, objective judgment of today’s realities, and with conscious actions for tomorrows possibilities.

    Our hands-on approach is direct, honest, informal and effective. As we strive to share our vision and extend our expertise to organizations to transform its way of working and to exceed customers’ expectations.

  • Foundation pillars.

    Every action and decision we make stems from the core of our beliefs. These are foundational pillars we heavily rely on, and are interconnected as they complement each other and are carried out sequentially.

  • Perspective

    The perspective we hold ensures us to unbiasedly analyse the current (market) situation, and provide unrestrained and honest feedback.

    This viewpoint allows us to recognize the situation for as it is, and lets us accept unmanageable external circumstances for what they are.

    In return, through this awareness, we can make conscious choices and decisions regarding how we react and build upon these uncontrollable factors.
